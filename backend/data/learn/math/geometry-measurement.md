## Perimeter

**Perimeter** is the **distance around** a shape.

- **Rectangle:** **P = 2 × (length + width)**
- **Square** (side **s**): **P = 4 × s**

## Area

**Area** is the **space inside** a flat shape (often in square units: cm², m²).

- **Rectangle:** **A = length × width**
- **Square:** **A = side × side**
- **Parallelogram:** **A = base × height** (height is **perpendicular** to the base)
- **Trapezoid** (parallel sides **a** and **b**, height **h**): **A = ½ × (a + b) × h**

## Circles (intro)

- **Radius (r):** center to edge  
- **Diameter:** **2r**  
- **Circumference** (around): **C ≈ π × diameter** or **2πr**  
- **Area:** **A ≈ πr²**  
Use **π ≈ 3.14** unless your teacher says otherwise.

## Volume (rectangular prism)

A **box** with length **L**, width **W**, height **H**:

**V = L × W × H** (cubic units: cm³, m³).
