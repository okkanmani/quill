## Whole-number operations

- **Addition / subtraction:** Line up place value; borrow or carry as needed.
- **Multiplication:** Know times tables through **12 × 12** if you can — they speed everything up.
- **Division:** Think “how many times does the divisor fit?” Remainders are okay when the question asks for them.

## Order of operations (PEMDAS / BEDMAS)

Work in this order:

1. **Brackets** (parentheses) first  
2. **Exponents** (powers) if any  
3. **Multiplication and division** — **left to right**  
4. **Addition and subtraction** — **left to right**

**Example:** **48 ÷ (6 + 2) × 3**  
Brackets first: **6 + 2 = 8** → **48 ÷ 8 × 3** → **6 × 3 = 18**.

## Mental math strategies

- **Split numbers:** **48 + 17 = 48 + 10 + 7**
- **Compensate:** **99 + 38 = 100 + 38 − 1**
- **Doubling / halving** for friendly multiplications
