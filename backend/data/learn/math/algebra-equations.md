## Equations

An **equation** says two expressions are equal. Often you **solve** for the unknown.

## Balance idea

Whatever you do to one side, do to the other to keep balance.

**Example:** **4x − 3 = 25**  
1. Add **3** to both sides: **4x = 28**  
2. Divide both sides by **4**: **x = 7**

**Example:** **3x + 9 = 33**  
1. Subtract **9**: **3x = 24**  
2. Divide by **3**: **x = 8**

## Checking

Plug your answer back in: **4(7) − 3 = 28 − 3 = 25** ✓
